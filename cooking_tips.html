Cooking tips

Feel free to find out for yourself this month's top cooking tips by hovering on the numbers.

Do you have trouble blending? Always put your liquids in your blender first—water, juice, broths—and then load your more solid ingredients—veggies, leafy greens, fruits.

1

No more crying over onions! Put onions in ice water for 10 minutes before cutting them; this prevents them from causing your eyes to water.

2

Would you like crispy fries? Slice the potato, then remove the starch by soaking in water for one hour before baking or frying.

3

Soggy leafy greens? To make leafy greens last longer, wrap them in damp paper towels and place in a sealable plastic bag before storing.

4

Even after washing with soap, you struggle to keep the cutting board clean? Disinfect wood cutting boards by hand washing it with vinegar.

5

Peel tomatoes with ease! Cut an X in the top, and then simmer in a pot of hot water for 15 to 30 seconds. Once it has cool down and the skin will fall right off.
